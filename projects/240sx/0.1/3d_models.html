3D Models

Models of the 240sx chassis and Spark EV components were created with 3D scanners.

240sx Chassis

The engine bay was scanned with the Revopoint POP scanner over multiple attempts. The entire engine bay was covered in masking tape, and alignment stickers were placed on the tape throughout the bay. The POP scans were prone to drift in positional accuracy over distances larger than a foot or so, so the entire bay was scanned a corner at a time, then merged together in post.

Figure 1. 240sx engine bay scan

The underbody was scanned with RPLidar based area scanner. The mesh shown below is a combination of 24 discrete scans.

Figure 2. 240sx underbody scan

An S13 240sx model was extracted from an Assetto Corsa addon as a CAD reference, and for future aerodynamic simulations with OpenFOAM. The model aligns reasonably well with the chassis scans.

Figure 3. Chassis model with scans

The model creates some reasonable looking renders as well.

Figure 4. 240sx model render

EV Components

The motor, transaxle, battery cell, and power electronics were all 3D scanned with the Revopoint POP.
